About Me

My Programming Journey

I have a deep love for programming and have been self-teaching myself different programming languages for years. What started as curiosity has evolved into a true passion for software development and problem-solving through code.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Network Administration, which has given me a strong foundation in understanding how systems work together. Beyond networking, I have a particular passion for learning the ins and outs of video game creation and production. The intersection of technology, creativity, and interactive storytelling fascinates me, and I'm constantly exploring new tools and techniques in game development.

My Love for Animals & the Outdoors

My passion isn't limited to technology—I also have a deep love for animals. I earned an Associate's degree in Veterinary Technician, which has allowed me to combine my care for animals with practical medical knowledge. Working with animals has taught me patience, compassion, and attention to detail.

I enjoy spending time outdoors, whether it's working in the garden, tending to plants and watching them grow, or hiking through the woods with my PSA, Cosmo. There's something incredibly peaceful about being in nature, and Cosmo is always the perfect companion for any adventure. These moments help me recharge and find balance in life.
